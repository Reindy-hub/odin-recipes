Description

this soup is mostly made from the finest groundnut fruit produce in Ghana Groudnut is the main recipe in the preparation of this dish.

Ingrediate

  • groundnut paste
  • smoked fish or meat
  • salt
  • tomatoes, pepper and onion
  • okro or garden eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. mix your groundnut paste with water and put it on fire for it to cook
  2. after few minutes of continue stirring, oil will be on top of it showing that it is ready
  3. steam your fish or meat and pour your groundnut paste on it
  4. add your blended pepper, onion and tomatoes
  5. add salt and your okro or garden eggs and allow it to boil for about 30 minutes
  6. your soup is ready to be served
